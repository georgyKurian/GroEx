How to create a New Account?

1.    Go to the Login page

2.    Click on “Sign up” tab

3.    Add your details to create a new account

4.    Click “Submit”

5.    Here, you can login to your new account.

 

 

How to create a New Group?        

1.    You need to login with GroEx account

2.    Click the Link New group.

3.    Here, you can set your group name, then submit.

4.    Click “Add” to add the number of group members.

5.    Then Click “Back”  to go to the Group settings page.

 

                                            

How to add a new member to the existing group?                                                  

1.    Login to your existing account.

2.    Click on the link with your existing group name.

3.    Go to the “Settings” tab, click on it.

4.    Select a member from the drop box.

5.    Then Click on the “Add”.

 

How to add a new bill?

1.    Click on your group name link in the login page.

2.    Go to the “Add Bill” tab on the top of the page.

3.    Here, you can add your bill type, description, amount & date.

4.    Then Click “Submit”

 

 

How to change your existing group details? 

1.    Go to the login page.

2.    Login with your valid username and password.

3.    Go to the “Settings” tab.

4.    Here you can change your group name and members.

5.    Then Click “Change”.

6.    Your changes will be updated.

 

How to view your expense graph using your existing transaction details?

1.    Login with your existing account

2.    Go to the “Expense graph” tab

3.    Here you can able to view your expense graph of your last 5 months

 

How to edit/delete expenses from the group?

1.    Login with your valid  username and password

2.    Click on the link displayed with your existing group name

3.    Here you can see your previous transaction details

4.    Click “Edit”

5.    Here you can change your transaction details

6.    And click “Submit”

7.    If you want to delete any previous transactions,

Click the “Delete” tab.

 

How to delete a group member?

1.    Login to your existing account

2.    Go to the “Settings” tab.

3.    Here you can see the group members

4.    You can delete group members by clicking cross bar

 

How to logout from your account?

1.    Go to the Login page

2.    Click on the “user” tab on the left corner of the navigation bar.

3.    Click on “Logout”
